Sach toh ye hai ki tum sirf meri girlfriend ya pyar nahi ho, tum meri aadat ban chuki ho. Wo aadat jo chhoot jaye toh saans lene mein bhi takleef ho. Kabhi-kabhi main baith kar purane dino ke baare mein sochta hoon jab tum meri life mein nahi thi. Tab bhi main jee to raha tha, par tab zindagi mein wo 'mahak' nahi thi jo ab hai. Tumne aakar mere har berang din mein rang bhar diye hain. Pata hai, tumhare saath hone se sab kuch kitna easy lagne lagta hai? Jab main thaka hua hota hoon, ya jab duniya ki baatein dimaag kharab karti hain, toh bas tumhare baare mein soch kar hi sukoon mil jata hai. Tumhara wo choti-choti baaton par hansna, wo bina wajah mujhse ladna, aur phir utne hi pyar se maan jana ye sab mere liye kisi therapy se kam nahi hai. Maine aksar logon ko kehte suna tha ki 'pyaar mein duniya badal jati hai,' tab main yakeen nahi karta tha. Par ab samajh aata hai. Ab jab main koi song sunta hoon, toh uske lafz tumhari yaad dilate hain. Jab koi khoobsurat cheez dekhta hoon, toh sabse pehle dil karta hai tumhe bataun. Main ye toh nahi jaanta ki aage kya hoga, lekin ye jaanta hoon ki jahan tum ho, wahi meri jagah hai. Tum meri wo 'home' ho jahan main din bhar ki thakan ke baad wapas aana chahta hoon. Main shayad har roz ye bol na paun, par tumhare bina main 'main' nahi rehta. Tum meri strength ho, meri sabse badi khushi ho, aur meri sabse pyari haqeeqat ho. Bas itna chahta hoon ki hum humesha aise hi rahein ek doosre ka sahara, ek doosre ki muskurahat. Kyunki tumhare bina ye safar kitna bhi suhana ho, mujhe adhoora hi lagega.
