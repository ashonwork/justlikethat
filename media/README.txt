HOW TO ADD YOUR PHOTOS AND VIDEOS:

1. Add your photos to the "photos" folder
   - Name your main couple photo as: couple.jpg (or .png)
   - You can add more photos with any name

2. Add your videos to the "videos" folder
   - Name your video as: memory.mp4 (or .mov, .webm)

3. The website will automatically use these files!

Supported formats:
- Photos: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .gif
- Videos: .mp4, .mov, .webm
